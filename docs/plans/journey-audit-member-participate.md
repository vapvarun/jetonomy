## Top 3 Gaps

1. **Post-card vote buttons are dead (blocker).** `post-card.php:42-44` renders upvote and downvote as `<span>` elements with no `data-wp-on` event wiring. CSS gives them `cursor:pointer` and hover styles so they look interactive, but clicking them does nothing. Votes on the post listing page are completely non-functional for non-privileged members.

2. **Feed space post creation blocked client-side on missing title (major).** `view.js:2408` hardcodes `requireTitle: true` inside `submitNewPost` regardless of `ctx.spaceType`. The server (`class-posts-controller.php:380-388`) correctly derives a title from the body for feed spaces and never requires one — but the client never reaches the server. Members posting a status update see "Please enter a title for your topic." and cannot submit.

3. **Downvotes fire "someone voted on your post" notifications (major).** `class-votes-controller.php:163` fires `jetonomy_after_vote` for every vote value including -1. `class-notifier.php:414-495` dispatches a `vote_on_post` notification with no value discrimination. Authors receive "X people voted on your post" after someone downvotes them — misleading and erodes trust in the notification inbox.
