# Product Overview Video Script

## Video Details
- **Title:** Jetonomy - The Modern Forum Plugin for WordPress
- **Duration:** 2:00-2:30
- **Purpose:** Launch video - show what Jetonomy does and why it matters
- **Tone:** Confident, friendly, show-don't-tell
- **Target:** Community Manager Chris, Small Business Owner Sam

---

## Script

### INTRO (0:00 - 0:15)

**VISUAL:** WordPress dashboard, cursor clicks Jetonomy in sidebar
**NARRATOR:** "Adding a forum to WordPress used to mean slow pages, ugly interfaces, and spending hours on moderation. Jetonomy changes that."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "The Modern Forum Plugin for WordPress"

### THE PROBLEM (0:15 - 0:30)

**VISUAL:** Split screen - left shows bloated wp_posts table, right shows dated bbPress UI
**NARRATOR:** "Most forum plugins store everything in the WordPress posts table. That works for 100 topics. At 10,000, your entire site slows down. And you are stuck manually moderating every new member."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "10,000 topics = slow WordPress"

### THE SOLUTION (0:30 - 1:00)

**VISUAL:** Jetonomy community home page - 3 categories, 10 spaces with emoji icons
**NARRATOR:** "Jetonomy uses 24 custom database tables. Your forum grows without touching wp_posts. Page loads stay under 200 milliseconds even at 50,000 topics."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "24 custom tables. Sub-200ms."

**VISUAL:** Topic page with trust level badges on reply cards
**NARRATOR:** "And moderation? Jetonomy handles it automatically. New members start restricted. As they contribute - posting, answering questions, earning upvotes - they advance through six trust levels. Top members can edit, moderate, and manage. You set the thresholds once."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "6 trust levels. Community moderates itself."

### KEY FEATURES (1:00 - 1:30)

**VISUAL:** Quick montage of screens - each shown for 3-4 seconds
**NARRATOR:** "Create forum spaces, Q&A boards, and idea channels. Threaded replies with voting. Full-text search with filters. Draft posts and scheduling. Built-in anti-spam with reCAPTCHA or Turnstile. Move, merge, and split topics. And a design that adapts to your theme automatically."

Screens to show:
1. Space listing with sort pills (Latest/Popular/Unanswered)
2. Q&A topic with accepted answer (green badge)
3. Search page with filter bar
4. New post form with draft/schedule split-button
5. Admin Anti-Spam settings tab
6. Move topic modal with space picker

### PRO TEASER (1:30 - 1:50)

**VISUAL:** Reaction emoji appearing on a reply, message thread, poll with vote bars
**NARRATOR:** "Jetonomy Pro adds emoji reactions, private messaging, polls, custom badges, analytics, and more. Everything your growing community needs."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "Jetonomy Pro - For communities that mean business."

### CLOSE (1:50 - 2:15)

**VISUAL:** Setup wizard - step 1, step 2, step 3 done in quick cuts
**NARRATOR:** "Get started in five minutes. Install, run the setup wizard, and your community is live. Free at wbcomdesigns.com."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "jetonomy.com" + "Download Free" button

**VISUAL:** Community home page with active content, scrolling
**NARRATOR:** "Jetonomy. Forums, Q&A, idea boards, and Show and Tell - built for communities that grow."

---

## Production Notes
- All visuals from the model site at forums.local (Jetonomy Demo + BuddyX)
- Background music: light, upbeat, modern (not EDM)
- No face-on-camera needed - screen recording with voiceover
- Export at 1080p, 16:9
- Thumbnail: community home page screenshot with "The Modern Forum Plugin" text overlay
